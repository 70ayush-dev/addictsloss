About the Story

After deciding to quit therapy and believing he's in a better place, Orion pushes his trauma deep down and moves forward with Anette. Together, they pursue their dreams of becoming a nurse and therapist, with Orion inspired to help others through his own experiences.

But when prescribed medication to help with his trauma sends him spiraling into a passive episode, Orion shuts everyone out. Despite Anette's unwavering support, his inability to express emotions and growing toxicity threatens to destroy everything they've built.

Haunted by his father's addiction and a toxic childhood, Orion's consequential actions throw his relationship into jeopardy. As his best friend fails to persuade him to stop his deadly path, a tragic heartbreak becomes inevitable.
